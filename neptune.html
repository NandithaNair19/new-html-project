NEPTUNE

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system. Known for its deep blue color,Neptune's atmosphere is primarily composed of hydrogen,helium,and methane,which gives it its characteristic hue.It has a strong magnetic field and extreme winds,which can reach speeds of over 2,000 kilometers per hour.Neptune has 14 known moons,with Triton being the largest and most notable.Triton is unique because it orbits Neptune in the opposite direction of the planet's rotation.Despite being so distant,Neptune has a dynamic and turbulent atmosphere,with storm systems that rival those on Jupiter.

Here are few features:

Feature Description
Distance from the Sun 4.5 billion kilometers
Diameter 49,244 kilometers
Orbital Period 165 Earth years
Day Length 16.11 Earth hours
Temperature -200°C,can range from -218°C to -200°C
Moons 14 known moons(Triton-the largest moon)
Atmosphere hydrogen (80%),helium (19%),methane(1%)
Rings Faint and dark ring system,consisting mostly of small,icy particles
Color Deep blue due to methane
